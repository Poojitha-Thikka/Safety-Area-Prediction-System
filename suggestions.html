Safety Suggestions

  • Stay in well-lit areas. Avoid walking through dark alleys or isolated places at night.
  • Use trusted transportation. Always use licensed or known transportation services for commuting.
  • Keep emergency numbers handy. Always have quick access to emergency contacts, including local authorities.
  • Be aware of your surroundings. Stay alert, and avoid distractions such as using your phone in unfamiliar areas.
  • Travel with friends or in groups. If possible, avoid traveling alone, especially in less secure areas.
